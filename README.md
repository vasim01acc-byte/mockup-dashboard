I recently sat down with a challenge: How do we help finance teams move from being "data rich" to "insight poor"? 

The answer wasn’t just more charts—it was a comprehensive Power BI Financial Intelligence Dashboard designed to transform raw numbers into a strategic roadmap.
Here is the story behind the build:

📍 The Pulse: Immediate Health Checks
Every great story needs a beginning. I started with five high-level KPIs, including Opex Ratio and Net Profit Retention. This provides an immediate pulse check on operational efficiency and liquidity, giving leadership the "Big Picture" in under 10 seconds.

📍 The Bridge: Explaining the "Why"
One of the biggest hurdles for finance leads is explaining the "leakage" between budget and actuals. To solve this, I built a Revenue Variance Bridge. Using Waterfall Analysis, this feature identifies exactly where favorable gains occurred and where adverse leakages slowed momentum.

📍 The Horizon: Moving Beyond the Rearview Mirror
Data shouldn't just show you where you've been; it should show you where you’re going. By leveraging historical averages, I integrated a 90-Day Cash Flow Projection. This visualizes the capital runway, ensuring future liquidity is never a guessing game.

📍 The Drill-Down: Spotting the Signal in the Noise
By clustering Revenue vs. Segment Profitability, the dashboard allows users to instantly spot departments that are high-volume but low-margin. It’s the difference between seeing a problem and having the data to make a targeted strategic adjustment in just two clicks.
